The Future of CandyCoil



Dear Coil Friends,
Thank you for your dedication to CandyCoil. We understand how important your hair health is to you.
We regret to inform you that after May of 2017 this site will be there will be no new additions to this site. We have had a great run and have enjoyed bringing you healthy hair content.
I want to personally thank each and every one of you as readers, viewers, subscribers for your interest in CandyCoil
In order to focus on future projects that I have lined up it would not be feasible to attempt to maintain this site.
I hope that CandyCoil served its purpose and that you were able to gain a better understanding when it comes to hair health.
If you wish, you may continue to use the tips and tricks already posted here as a guide for your hair Journey. We understand that this may come as shocking news, but do not fret.
There are many resources that can be used in order to find the hair information that you seek.
If the closing of this site inspires you to create your own, moves you to reach out to other members of the natural hair community I encourage you to do so.
I only want to deliver content to you all that has my full focus. I thank you for your time and patience and wish each of you all the best in your future endeavors.
For more natural hair care tips and tricks I encourage you to visit youtube.
