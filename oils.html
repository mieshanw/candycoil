Oils

Incorporating essential and carrier oils into your hair regimine can have many benefits.
Oils are known to be a great way to moisturize your locks.

Carrier oils are lightweight oils that can be used with essential oils in order to carry moisture to your hair.
Carrier oils can be used in order to penetrate the hair shaft with moisture, or on top of a moisturizer in order to seal moisture in.

Essential oils are more potent than their carrier friends.
Essential oils are known to be very concentrated and you only need a few drops. They are not used to seal in moisture but each have their own benefits.
