Hair Types

There are many topics in the natural hair community in which naturalista's can not agree on, hair typing just happens to be one of those topics.
But either way; we at Candycoil are here to ensure you understand the different types of hair types, as well as the terminology, look, feel, and how to determine your own hair type
Hair typing is deemed as touchy topic due to the bias and discrimination that tighter hair textures are subjected to by hair care companies, the media, and other natural hair community members
We understand why some may choose to not aknowledge hair typing but we believe that knowing your hair type can help you in selecting the products that work best for you on your natural hair care journyey.
Hair types include Type 2, Type, 3 and Type 4 hair. Each number representing the texture of the hair :wavy, curly, coarse and then divded by letter (a,b,c) to represent the behaviour / feel.
Many women find that their hair includes multiple hair types. It is not uncommon for your hair to have different hair types in certain sections
Some woman find that the back of their hair is Type 4 while the front is type 3. This should not concern you
It is important to understand your hair and how it grows, it is possible that your different hair types may be due to heat damage, head damage is not healthy.

Hair Types

Type 2

Wavy hair
2a - slighly wavy hair, sticks close to the head. No natural sheen, lacks volume.
2b - s shaped waves, slighly frizzier than 2a .
2c - coarse wavy hair.

Type 3

Curly hair
3a - Definite S patterned curls, natural sheen.
3b - Voluminous curls, may be coarse or dense. No natural sheen.
3c - tight corkscrew curls , with the circumfrence of a pencil or a straw.

Type 4

Coarse hair
4a - tightly coil S pattern curls / spirals.
4b - Z shapoed coils , less defined curl pattern. Tily coiled and wirey feel.
4c - can range from fine to course, tighly kinked with little to no definition. Lots of shrinkage.

It is important to know that even though hair types exist, they do not exist to exlude one type out .
Every hair type is beautiful and in order to maintain heathly natural hair you must embrace your own personal texture.
