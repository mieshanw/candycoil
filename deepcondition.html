Weak and frizzy hair are just a few examples of the many symptoms of dry hair. Many women struggle combating dry hair especially in the colder months.
When your hiar is not receiving enough moisture or is not able to retain that moisture adequately you may be left with dry and brittle hair. There are many ways to fight back against dry hair. Pre-Poo
You shampoo your hair in order to clean out the built up dirt and particles on your scalp and hair follicles. Even though shampooing has many benefits for the hair shampooing too often can also be detremintal.
The pre poo method allows you to still cleanse your hair while also adding moisture o it. Instead of washing your hair with shampoo try washing it with your favorite moisturizing conditioner.
You will notice the instant slip that it gives your hair. Limit your pre-poo to once a week for the best benefits, pre-pooing too often can also cause your hair to weaken.

Deep Conditioning
Deep conditioning your hair allows moisture to deeply penetrate the hair shaft.
  • Add essential oils to your favorite conditioner.
  • Apply the conditioner and oil mixture generously to your hair, avoiding your scalp.
  • Place a shower cap or plastic grocery bag over your hair for at least 30 minutes before you rinse.

Applying heat to your deep conditioner will open your hair folicles allowing more moisture to penetrate your hair.
You can do this by placing a warm towel over your shower cap/ plastic bag, or by sitting under a hooded dryer.

You can deep condition your hair using your favorite regular conditioner or conditioner marketed solely towards deep conditioning treatments at your local beauty supply store.
You can also make your own deep conditioner using your choice of butters and oils.
